Famous Landmarks in Colombia

1. Monserrate

Monserrate is a mountain that dominates the city center of Bogotá, the capital of Colombia. It is a popular pilgrimage site and offers stunning views of the city.

2. Ciudad Perdida (The Lost City)

Ciudad Perdida is an ancient city in the Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta. It is believed to have been founded around 800 CE, making it older than Machu Picchu.

3. Caño Cristales

Known as the "River of Five Colors," Caño Cristales is famous for its vibrant colors caused by aquatic plants. It is located in the Serranía de la Macarena province.
